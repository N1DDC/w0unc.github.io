About Us

The Amateur Radio Club of UNC Charlotte is a student interest group whose purposes are to provide the local community with emergency communications, educate students, faculty, and staff in becoming amateur radio operators, and to foster fellowship within the amateur radio community. This is registered student organization on the campus of UNC Charlotte, and it is a fledgling group.

Information


Repeater

We have a repeater on the roof of the library broadcasting with on frequency 442.650MHz (+5MHz) 88.5 tone. We stress that you must be an FCC Licensed Operator (or hold a comparable authorization) to access our repeater.


Meetings

We meet monthly at a predesignated time and place. Check our NinerEngage page for meeting announcements. Any undergraduate, graduate, alumni, or faculty may attend the meetings.


Contact

The easiest way to contact us is though the repeater, you can also contact us by emailing club@w0unc.org.

The easiest way to get involved with our club is to come to a meeting! If you cannot make our regular meetings, you can also send an email to club@w0unc.org or contact us though our repeater.
