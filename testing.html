FCC Registration Number (FRN)

It is highly recommended that you get an FRN number before attending a testing session. It can be used in place of a Social Security Number and in some cases it will be the only number you will be allowed to use. Remember to write it down or store it someplace safe after you receive it.

Upcoming Sessions

We are currently not offering our own testing sessions. A few of our members are undergoing VE training and we will begin scheduling our own sessions once they are done. We will include unaffiliated sessions if a sizable amount of our members are attending or transportation is provided.

Exams marked as 'Transportation Provided' will have the event time, not the departure time. You are responsible for contacting us in order to get the details about departure time and location, or it will be provided in the RSVP reply email.
